Why a Paper Wallet?

Paper wallets are a secure and efficient method to store Bitcoin on the Liquid Network offline. You can store or gift BTC seamlessly without ever needing an internet connection by treating these wallets like physical cash.

Cost-Effective Gifting

Paper wallets provide a simple and cost-effective way to gift l-sats (Liquid sats). They enable future transactions to be conducted at a lower cost, making them a great option for introducing friends and family to the world of Bitcoin and Liquid assets.

Secure Asset Storage

With a paper wallet, you can securely store various assets, including L-BTC and L-USDt (Tether). This offline storage method protects your assets from online threats such as hacking, phishing, and malware, ensuring peace of mind.

Long-Term Security

Paper wallets are immune to hardware failures, software corruption, and other technical issues that can affect digital wallets. As long as the physical paper is kept safe, your assets remain secure.

and.. Easy to create and use!

Creating a paper wallet is straightforward and doesn't require advanced technical knowledge. Simply print the wallet and use the mnemonic phrase to import it into a compatible digital wallet when needed.

Are you ready?

  1. Disconnect your device from the internet.
  2. Click "Generate New Wallet."
  3. Print your wallet and delete it from your device (including the recycle bin).
  4. Gift some sats to someone you love!

How to restore a Liquid PaperWallet?

You will find a mnemonic phrase printed on your Liquid PaperWallet. Simply import it into your favorite wallet to spend your l-sats! Below are two wallets that support our PaperWallets.

Green Wallet

Blockstream Green is an industry-leading Bitcoin wallet that offers you an unrivaled blend of security and ease-of-use. Blockstream Green is an open source, multi-platform Bitcoin wallet. Download now for iOS, Android or Desktop.

Marina

Marina is the ultimate gateway designed to access the Liquid Network directly from your browser. Receive and send Liquid Bitcoin, Tether and other Liquid assets. Marina is a Browser Extension supported by Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox.
